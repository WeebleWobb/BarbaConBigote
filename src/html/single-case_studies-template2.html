XYREM XTREME
Agents of Change.

Introducing gamification to improve user engagement.

Using gamification we set out to create an experience that could be engaging, rewarding and fun. On this marketing campaign we incorporated gamification techniques to increase user engagement and a feeling of empowerment. The use of Blue and Grey are showcased to evoke the feeling of trust, innovation, and progression. Simple hard edges with vertical and horizontal lines are complemented by complex forms and shapes that evoke modernism, change, security, strength, and precision.

01.

Initial Campaign

To begin, we created a simple email blast that would be sent to all sales representatives. This email was ment to mimic a top secret message alert commonly seen in spies or agents. By establiushing the aesthetic early, we were able to create a landing page that would not only reinforce the look and feel by most importantly provide the “Mission Brief”. The purpose was to constently create a feling of importance, excitement, and gamification.

02.

Mission Phase Emails

Once the campaign was up and rolling and all reps understood the mission, periodical Phase emails would be sent to provide Challenges. Each email circulated around a Theme that reinfoces the sense of urgency and comitment.

03.

Agents of Change Rep Portal

The Rep portal was created to keep track of Sales Rep performance by region. By allowing each “Agent” to easily access performance information, we created a playing field for competition.

04.

Agents of Change Update Emails

Not only were Reps able to see region performance via Rep Portal, but periodic update emails would be sent out to track region performance. By implementing badges for performance, we further pushed the reward system and gamification.
